Recommended books to read

Miracle Morning

The core idea of the Miracle Morning is to dedicate the first hour of your day to personal growth and self-improvement.

Emotional intelligence

Emotional intelligence is the ability to recognize, understand, and manage your own emotions, as well as the emotions of others. It's essential for building strong relationships and achieving success.

Sense and Sensibility

It is a timeless tale of love, loss, and the importance of finding a balance between reason and emotion. It is a beloved classic that continues to resonate with readers today.

Emma

It is a witty and insightful novel that explores themes of class, gender, and the complexities of human relationships. It is one of Jane Austen's finest works and continues to be enjoyed by readers today.

Literature review

World classic literature encompasses a vast collection of literary works that have stood the test of time, transcending cultural and historical boundaries.
